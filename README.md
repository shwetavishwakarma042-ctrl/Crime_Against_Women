# Crime Against Women Dashboard

**Power BI Project by Shweta Vishwakarma**

---

## Overview
This Power BI dashboard analyzes **crime against women in India from 2001 to 2014**.  
The dashboard provides **key insights**, **trends over time**, and **state-wise distribution** of different crime types.

**Key Features:**

- **KPI Cards:** Quick metrics for Total Crimes, Rape, Dowry Deaths, and Kidnapping  
- **Trend Analysis:** Line chart showing crime trends over 14 years  
- **Crime Type Comparison:** Stacked column chart comparing different crime types  
- **Top 10 States:** Bar chart showing states with highest total crimes  
- **State-wise Map:** Visualizing crime distribution across India  
- **Crime Type Pie Chart:** Proportional distribution of crime types  

---

## Dataset
- Source: Kaggle / Government of India crime data  
- File: `Crimes_Against_Women_2001-2014.csv`  
- Columns include:  
  - Assault on women with intent to outrage her modesty  
  - Cruelty by husband or his relatives  
  - Dowry deaths  
  - Kidnapping and abduction  
  - Rape  
  - Insult to modesty of women  
  - Importation of girls  
  - District  
  - State/UT  
  - Year

## How to Use
1. Open the `Crimes_Against_Women_Dashboard.pbix` file in **Power BI Desktop**.  
2. Use **slicers** for Year and State/UT to filter and explore data.  
3. Click on visuals to **interact and drill down** for insights.
